Extractors:

    Are most often used to extract parameters that need to be passed to subsequent requests for instance because of:

    https://pl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cross-site_request_forgery
    https://www.tinfoilsecurity.com/blog/what-is-cross-site-request-forgery-csrf

    Often we use them in assertions too.

    Most commonly used extractors:

    XpathExtractor
    CSSExtractor
    Regular Expression Extractor
    NEW (v4.0): Boundary Extractor - simplification of Regular Expression Extractor
